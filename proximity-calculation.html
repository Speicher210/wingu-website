Here are answers to your most important questions 

Do I need a credit card to start?

For the free trial you do not need a credit card. Just register with your Email and get things going.

Can I cancel my account at any time?

Yes you can. We will cancel your account at the end of the subscription period, even if you canceled one day before.

Do I need an App to start?

To see how wingu works, you can use our showcase App available in the iOS and Android Appstores.

Can you build me my own App?

Yes we can. You can order your very own App as a whitelable version. As it has limited functionality, we recommend to build your own App and just use our SDK.

Do I need Beacons to try wingu?

No you don´t. You can start with a Geofence or QR-Code to understand how wingu works.

Where can I buy Beacons or NFC chips?

We are working with the best manufacturers in Europe. Just drop us a message and we get you in contact with them.

Can I add more than 1000 triggers?

Yes you can. Contact us for a special pricing in your Enterprise account.

I have more questions. Do you have more answers?
