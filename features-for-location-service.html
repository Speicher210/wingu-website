It´s all about features

We enable you to create proximity triggers and location based content and interaction without writing code. With our award winning web-interface you can create Geofences, Beacons, NFC and QR-Codes and attach rich content and interactions to it.

 

Configure locations and trigger as you go

We provide all major proximity triggers in one single platform. Beacons, Geofences, NFC or QR-Codes are easily configured via the web-interface - even by non-developers.

Beacon

Add and configure Beacons of almost every manufacturer. Just add the settings and you can start attaching content.

NFC

Configure NFC chips with our specific target URL and connect them to your content.

Geofence

Create ,configure and manage Geofences via our web interface. Change the position or settings at any time.

QR-code

Generate QR Codes and attach your landing page. The easiest way to attach content to a product.

 
 
 

CMS

With our intuitive rich content CMS and our wide set of features in modern design we allow you to create your own content and interactions and attach it to location triggers. We offer multi language content delivery, time setting, form cards and more.

 

Text

Catchy headlines, easy-to-remember lists or full texts: You do the writing and we put your content in the limelight.

Image gallery

From illustrations to photos, you upload your images individually or create a gallery for users to swipe through.  

Audio

Listen up! You upload your sounds and music as mp3 files, which are played at the touch of a button.

Rating

How many stars will you get for your service? Just ask your audience. Our rating template is ready to go.

Youtube

You copy and paste the link from your YouTube video, instantly generating a preview of the video. There is space for an optional short description.

Voucher / Coupons

Create a voucher in your own design or insert a valid EAN barcode: a great way to promote that summer sale and boost your point of sale!

HTML

If you want to bring content to another level. For devs only.

Webhook

Trigger 3rd party applications, like digital signage or your internal apps.

Brandbar

Show who you are. The easiest way to brand your content with your logo.

Form Fields

Create your own form and push data directly to your backend systems.

Assettracking

Use the out of the box asset tracking to locate your assets indoor and outdoor.

Batch Operations

Select and configure hundreds of triggers at once to save time.

Multilanguage

Be multinational. Push content in several languages via the wingu CMS.

 

Get insides with wingu analytics

Get to know your users and optimize your content and interaction. With wingu analytics you learn about reach, dwell time, routes etc. - to make sure, that you get the best out of your content and optimize the user experience.
