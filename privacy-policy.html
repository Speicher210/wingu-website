2. Legislative Basis and Processing

The processing of personal data requires a legal basis, which we would like to present to you here. For the processing of personal data, for which we have obtained the data subject's consent, the legal basis is Article 6(1)(a) of the General EU Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). 

For the processing of personal data, which is necessary for the performance of a contract to which the data subject is a party, GDPR Article 6(1)(b) serves as the legal basis. This also includes processing operations that are necessary to carry out pre-contractual measures. 

Insofar as the processing of personal data is required to fulfill a legal obligation to which our company is subject, GDPR Article 6 paragraph 1 lit. c  serves as the legal basis. 

If processing is necessary to safeguard a legitimate interest of our company, or of a third party, and if the interests, fundamental rights and freedoms of the data subject do not outweigh the former interest, GDPR Article 6(1)(f) serves as the legal basis for processing. The legitimate interest of our company lies in the performance of our business activities and in the analysis, optimisation and maintenance of the security of our online offer.

 

II. Log Files, Hosting

The server statistics automatically store data that the browser transmits to us within the framework of our legitimate interest  concerning analysis and for security reasons (so-called "log files").

The following data is transmitted to us via log files:

●     Language and version of the browser software 

●     Operating system and user interface

●     Referrer URL (the website you visited prior)

●     Host name of the device (IP address)

●     Date and time of the server request (time zone difference to GMT)

●     Transmitted data volume

The data will solely be used for statistical analysis to help us improve our security, our website and our offers. As a general rule we cannot connect individual people with the transmitted data. We do not undertake a consolidation of this data with other data sources. 

The data will be deleted within 7 days after a statistical evaluation. Data which further storage is required for evidentiary purposes are excluded from deletion until the respective incident has been clarified.

We use hosting services. These serve to provide infrastructure and platform services, computing capacity, storage space and database services, security services as well as technical maintenance services in order to maintain the operation of this online offer.

We and our hosting provider process inventory data, contact data, content data, contract data, usage data, meta- and communication data of customers, interested parties and visitors of this online offer on the basis of our legitimate interests in an efficient and secure provision of this online offer.

 

III. Contractual Processing

The personal data provided by you for contractual purposes, such as name, address or e-mail address, will only be used internally to answer your inquiries, process your orders or provide you access to certain contractual information. 

 

IV. Contacting wingu

When you contact us by e-mail or via the contact form, your details will be stored by us (and Zendesk in case the contact form is used) to answer your questions. A transfer to third parties (potentially apart from Zendesk) does not take place unless applicable data protection regulations justify a transfer or we are legally obliged to do so. You can revoke your consent at any time with effect for the future. In the event of revocation, your data will be deleted immediately, unless there is a legal exception for further processing. Your data will otherwise be deleted when we have processed your request or the purpose of storage has ceased and there are no other conflicting legal exceptions. You can inform yourself at any time about the data stored about your person.

 

V. Cookies

Our website uses Cookies. Cookies are small text files that are saved on your computer. Through cookies we receive information which serves to enhance your user experience and/or simplifies the navigation on our website. 

It is possible to use our website without accepting cookies. You can configure your browser settings in such a manner that will prevent the setting of third party, or all cookies. You can also delete saved cookies. In case you do not accept cookies, please be aware that our website and portal might not function error-free.

For more information about third party cookies, please read the paragraphs concerning Google Analytics, Facebook, Twitter and Zendesk.

 

VI. Newsletter

If you would like to receive the newsletter offered on our website, we need a valid e-mail address from you, which allows us to check that you are the owner of the given e-mail address or that the owner agrees to receive the newsletter. 

After providing your e-mail address, we will send you a confirmation e-mail to the address provided, asking you to confirm that you would like the newsletter to be sent. If you do not confirm this within 24 hours, your data relating to the newsletter dispatch will be deleted automatically. If you confirm your wish to receive the newsletter, we will store your e-mail address until you cancel the newsletter.

We only send newsletters with your consent or a legal permission. You can revoke your consent to the storage of the data, the e-mail address as well as their use for sending the newsletter at any time. You will find a link to cancel the newsletter at the end of each newsletter. If users have only subscribed to the newsletter and cancelled their subscription, their personal data will be deleted.

 

VII. Registration

Our website offers a registration option. The personal data entered during registration will be communicated to the controller. The data are stored exclusively for the purpose of internal use. During registration, the user's IP address and the date and time of registration are stored. This serves to prevent misuse of the services. The data will not be passed on to third parties. An exception exists if there is a legal obligation to pass on.

Registration of the data is required for the provision of content or services. Registered persons have the possibility to delete or modify the stored data at any time. The person concerned receives information about the personal data stored about him or her at any time.

We pass on data to third parties if this is necessary for the fulfillment of the contract and/or we are legally obliged and / or entitled to do so in individual cases.

The IP address is therefore required for the display of this content. Third-party providers can also use so-called pixel tags (invisible graphics, also "web beacons") for statistical or marketing purposes. Pixel tags can be used to evaluate information such as visitor traffic on the pages of this website. The pseudonymous information may also be stored in cookies on the user's device and may contain technical information similar to logfiles. Below we list our service providers. 3.1 Google Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics. Google Analytics is a web analysis service of Google LLC, 1600 Amphitheatre Parkway Mountain View, CA 94043, USA (Google). Google may also transfer this information to third parties if this is required by law or if third parties process this data on behalf of Google. IX. Rights of the Data Subject

You have a right to information concerning your personal data stored by us. According to the legal regulations you also have a right to correction of incorrect data, blocking, data transferability and deletion of your personal data. Send us an e-mail with the subject "Privacy".
You also have a right of appeal to a data protection supervisory authority if you believe that the processing of your personal data is in violation of the law. 


If you have given your consent, you have the right to revoke your consent at any time. This does not affect the legality of the processing carried out on the basis of the consent until revocation. You can object to the future processing of your personal data in accordance with the legal requirements at any time. The objection may be lodged in particular against processing for direct marketing purposes.

 

X. Data Retention and Deletion

We store personal data for the duration of the respective legal retention period or as long as the purpose of the collection exists. After expiry of the retention period, the data is routinely deleted, unless there is a need for the initiation of a contract or for performance of the contract. If the user's data are not deleted because they are necessary for other, and legally permissible, purposes, their processing is limited as far as possible. Accordingly, the data will be blocked as far as possible and not processed for other purposes. This applies, for example, to user data that must be retained for commercial or tax reasons.

 

XI. Security Information

We make great efforts to process your personal data by taking all technical and organizational possibilities in such a way that the regulations of the data protection laws are observed and in order to protect these data. Our website or the communication with us via our website is encrypted via HTTPS.
